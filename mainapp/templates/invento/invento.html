Born in the year 2012, INVENTO is the national level annual techno-management festival of Government Engineering College Palakkad, Kerala. The affair calls for indefinite hard work of the students to bring about technological and cultural brilliance upon the institute. Hosted in the month of May it lays premise for numerous activities such as events, workshops, projects, presentations and exhibitions. Since its establishment, INVENTO has been followed up in the years 2016, 2017 and 2018 whose appreciable success opened doors to coming years as well. During its lifetime the techfest has managed to expand the knowledge zone and inculcate innovative ideas in students, deliver marks of excellence to the foundation and fulfil the responsibility of emerging engineers towards the society.
