About Gec Sreekrishnapuram



Government Engineering College Sreekrishnapuram is a premier educational institute established in 1999. It is situated in Palakkad district of Kerala. The college is fully owned by Govt.of Kerala and is under the Directorate of Technical Education, Kerala. GEC Sreekrishnapuram is approved by the AICTE and is affiliated to APJ Abdul kalam Technological University (from 2015 onwards).

The college offers 4-year full-time B.Tech courses in the following branches:1) Computer Science and Engineering 2) Electronics and Communication Engineering 3)Information Technology 4)Mechanical Engineering 5)Electrical and Electronics Engineering. It also conducts an M.Tech course in Computational Linguistics in the Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering from 2011 onwards.Institution offers PHD under KTU since 2016 onwards.

About Invento



Born in the year 2012, INVENTO is the national level annual techno-management festival of Government Engineering College Palakkad, Kerala. The affair calls for indefinite hard work of the students to bring about technological and cultural brilliance upon the institute. Hosted in the month of March it lays premise for numerous activities such as events, workshops, projects, presentations and exhibitions. Since its establishment, INVENTO has been followed up in the years 2016 and 2017 whose appreciable success opened doors to INVENTO18 as well. During its lifetime the techfest has managed to expand the knowledge zone and inculcate innovative ideas in students, deliver marks of excellence to the foundation and fulfil the responsibility of emerging engineers towards the society.
